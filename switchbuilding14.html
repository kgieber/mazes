How might you make a momentary switch* out of the following materials?

  • 3 - 2" x 2" pieces of cardboard
  • 4 pieces of soft foam
  • aluminum foil
  • tape (electrical, duct, or masking)
  • electrical wire (22- or 24-gauge)
  • scissors
  • wire strippers

*A momentary switch is one that only stays on while you are pressing it. Once you lift your hand, it goes back to its "off" state.
