Making Switches for the Arrow Keys

Here are some steps you can do to make your own set of switches to use in place of the computer's arrow keys.
What you'll need:

  • A flat board that doesn't conduct electricity. A piece of cardboard or foamcore work well.
  • aluminum foil
  • tape (electrical, duct, or masking)
  • electrical wire (22- or 24-gauge)
  • scissors
  • wire strippers
  • small nuts and screws - #8-32 x 3/4" (optional)
