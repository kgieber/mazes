Let’s Make a Switch

Glossary

Alligator Clip

A pinchable metal clip attached to a wire for connecting the wire to a battery or other object.

*Battery

A container that stores energy until it is needed.

Electric Circuit

Paths for moving energy.

Current

Electrical energy that is moving.

Electric

Something that works by or produces electricity.

*Electricity

The flow of tiny particles called electrons and protons, also known as energy.

Foamcore

Material made out of paper and foam used in art and for making scale models.

Gauge

The thickness of a wire.

Momentary Switch

A switch that only stays on while you are pressing it. Once you lift your hand, it goes back to its "off" state.

Switch

An object for opening and closing an electric circuit.

Wire Strippers

A tool used to strip the plastic coating from electric wires.

*Definitions from kids.britannica.com
